Hi, I'm Ryan.

Ryan Productions creates unique films and videos in Glen Allen, Virginia, and Bradford, Pennsylvania. The owner of Ryan Productions, Ryan Grandy, has over 17 years of experience in audio and video production. He helped start his high school TV station and interned at radio station 1490 WESB in Bradford, Pennsylvania.
Ryan worked in radio for five years and earned a bachelor's degree in communications from Edinboro University. He worked for one of the top wedding and event companies on the east coast for five years, then became a freelance videographer and DJ. He has worked on movie sets and TV commercials in Los Angeles and DJed in front of thousands of people. He's now concentrating on producing beautiful wedding films and great video content from commercials to live streaming events.
